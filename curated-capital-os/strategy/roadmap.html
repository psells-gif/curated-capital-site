Executive Summary

The Curated Capital Platform (working title: "Client Portal") is a subscription-based web application that provides real-time valuations and portfolio tracking for luxury asset collectors.

Strategic Context

The Opportunity: Curated Capital is building a lending business focused on a relatively untapped asset class‚Äîultra high value personal property (watches, jewelry, art, gold, collectibles). This market is underserved by traditional lenders who won't accept these assets as collateral, and poorly served by pawn shops that offer unfair valuations and a stigmatized experience.

The Vision: Build a $500M+ loan book providing liquidity against ultra high value items, plus a secondary line of business in buying/selling these items from the same customer base‚Äîwhich may be equally or more profitable than lending.

The Strategic Insight: Instead of only meeting customers at their moment of need (like competitor Borro.com does), we believe we can build long-term, sticky relationships by solving a problem they have today: the lack of visibility into what their collection is worth and the difficulty of transacting (lending/selling).

The Platform Strategy: If we successfully establish a low-cost subscription platform that solves the "inventory management and insights" need, we will have also built:

  • A large, qualified customer base (we know what they own)
  • High-margin subscription revenue
  • Sticky customer relationships
  • The trusted lender/buyer of choice when they need liquidity
Primary Purpose: Customer acquisition for Curated Capital's lending and acquisition businesses.

Validation Thesis

If we can acquire 1,000 paying subscribers during the OCCC licensing period (Feb‚ÄìAug 2026), we have validated product-market fit and should scale aggressively post-license.

Timeline

6-month validation window aligned with OCCC approval timeline.

1. Product Vision

The Problem

Affluent collectors who own at least $250K‚Äì$500K+ in watches, jewelry, art, and gold think of these assets as long-term investments‚Äîoften listing them on financial statements and insurance policies‚Äîbut have no way to see what they're worth today or ease of transacting (lending/selling). They hold conviction without actionable data.

The Solution

A portfolio dashboard that treats luxury assets like financial assets:

  • See real-time market valuations
  • Track performance over time
  • Transact easily‚Äîsell to us or borrow against assets (post-OCCC)

  • Patrick/Krister manually research and enter valuation
  • Good for first 100 beta users to validate UX

Phase 2 (Launch): Semi-Automated

CategoryApproach
WatchesMatch reference number to Chrono24/WatchCharts data (scraped or manual)
JewelryMatch brand + collection to resale comps (manual initially)
ArtMatch artist + title to auction records (manual initially)
GoldAutomated: Spot price API √ó weight

Valuation Display

  • Show: Current value, Low/High range (if available)
  • Show: Sources (e.g., "Based on Chrono24, WatchCharts")
  • Show: Last updated timestamp
  • Show: Confidence indicator (High/Medium/Low)

Manual Override

  • User can set custom value
  • Custom value flagged in UI
  • Custom value excluded from automated updates
